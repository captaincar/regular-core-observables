THETA PHASE RESULTS — 1+1D Dragan-Ekert Framework
============================================================

Key finding:
In 1+1D, both superluminal branches are related by PT,
which is a symmetry of the scalar/fermion action.
Therefore theta = 0 identically.

This does NOT constrain theta in 1+3D because:
- In 1+1D, superluminal boosts ARE symmetries (group closes)
- In 1+3D, superluminal boosts are NOT symmetries
